COSMIC CAMpAigN (2024)

Purple Pig

COSMIC CAMpAigN is a game that I have worked on from November 2023 to June 2024 with 3 other friends from the Girls in the Game! program. It is an adventure, story-driven game about a pig that accidentally embarks on a galactic journey in order to help the Chicken God - Edward save the galaxy from destruction.
The game was shown publicly and playtested during numerous events, such as:
- "Kolejna konferencja" - indie games conference in Bydgoszcz (April, 2024)
- "ZTGK Competition" - game design competition in Łódź (June, 2024)
- "PGA indie zone" - one of the biggest game conferences in Europe (October, 2024)
- "AGH Games Hall" - game dev conference for university students in Kraków (March, 2025)
During the ZTGK competition the game has won a special award from 11 bit studios.

Game available at: https://nartyna.itch.io/cosmic-campaig

My responsibilites in the project included:

  • Gathering references for the levels
  • Creating block-outs that support storytelling
  • Implementing the narrative with proper timing
  • Collecting and implementing on the levels public domain
    assets (3D models and sounds)
  • English translation of the game

Levels created for the game

1. Farm

This is a tutorial level of the game. The piggy dreams about a calm, careless life that is depicted on the level. It is structured as a sandbox in which the player can freely move and learn basic mechanics (walking, interacting with objects).
Additional challenge was that the level needed to match the initial cutscene that was already done.

2. The Slaughterhouse

The Slaughterhouse consists of 2 stages:
1. Runaway sequence
2. Walk And Talk Sequence
The first stage is very tense, the player is hit with the change of the main character looks and the necessity to run away from becoming a sausage.
I used light cues to slightly guide the player on this part of the level.

The second stage of the level acts as an introduction to the story.
It is a rather slow-paced walk and talk sequence in which we can listen to the voice in our head explaining us our mission.

In the sequence there is a vista seen from uphill depicting other slaughterhouses - showing the player how tiny they are and how cruel they are treated.
The sequence ends when the player reaches the rocketship that has landed during the sequence and acts as a landmark for the rest of the sequence.

3. The Rocket

Player enters the rocket and is introduced to the god Edward.
The rocket is filled with screens to show that there is no escape from Edward even though the spaceship is quite large and tall.
The level plays a filler role and is an attempt to make watching cutscenes more enjoyable and engaging.

4. The Spacemap

Edward asks piggy to fill the pilot's shoes.
Player starts a sequence in which they control the spaceship and need to beat an arcade galaxy level.


The moving controls are a bit off - they're simulating a pig flying a spaceship after all.


There are obstacles in the form of pieces of cheese - either moving in a particular way or falling from above.


The bubbles add to or take from the rocket's speed.

5. The Maze

The Maze is the game's last level. The player needs to wander through the circular maze in order to find peculiar ingredients and put them in the cauldron located in the centre.

To help player navigate the level, I made the maze circular shape, so that it is easier to get back to the centre.

I've also added beams of lights in places where the ingredients are located (they dissapear when we throw the ingredient into the cauldron
