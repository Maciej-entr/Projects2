DEREK (2022)

Lazy Coast Games

DEREK is the second game of The Lazy Coast Games indie studio located in Kołobrzeg that I have worked on. It is a retro-stylized, 1 BIT, speed runner puzzle platform game. You have just started your job as a pizza delivery man when you receive your first order from a rather eccentric lighthouse keeper. You arrive and put the salami pizza on his round table, but then something goes wrong. As it turns out that was not a table but a sacred machine that the lighthouse keeper was working on. You get sucked into another dimension and need to search for the missing cogs to get back and fix the machine.

Game available at: https://lazycoastgames.itch.io/derek

Sample of my work

Level 6 - "There is no life in the void"

difficulty: easy

The level is 6/26 which makes it one of the "onboarding" levels. The map isn't bigger than the viewport, which makes the level easy to navigate and analyze. In order to solve the level, the player needs to collect the key located in the bottom right corner of the level. The player is learning how to move boxes in order to complete the level. DEREK is a game that provides space for speedrunning and because of that you can also complete the level without moving the box - instead you can sprint in and out for the key without being caught by "the void".

Level 18 - "Oh it's useless"

difficulty: medium

This level is approximately in the middle of the game. The player has just been introduced to new opponents and gameplay mechanics such as the skulls, bubbles and mirrors which are all used together on the level. This level is medium difficulty - the tense music and the skull following the player at the beginning build the feeling of urgency. The level is not that difficult to pass as you are able to foresee most events but it's harder to master it because of the size of the level and the amount of elements. Some of them are there only to mislead the player (the vases and the second lock that doesn't have a key) - "Oh, it's useless".

Level 26 - "It was an accident almost the same as Derek"

difficulty: hard!

Closer to the end of the game we have introduced one last mechanic: double Dereks. This level is penultimate which means that the player already has completed a few "double" levels. Level 26 requires incredible focus to complete it - there are a lot of places that need thinking through/calculating or waiting out.
