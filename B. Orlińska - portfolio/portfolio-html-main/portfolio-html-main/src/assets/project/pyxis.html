PYXIS (2021)

Lazy Coast Games

PYXIS is the name of the first game that I have worked on. It was made during one school year by an idependent youth game studio (with members aged 11-18) located in Kołobrzeg - Lazy Coast Games. The game is written for the Game Boy Classic which makes it very unique - not everyone writes for retro platforms these days.

The story takes place in 2062 where after huge climate disasters, humanity falls into a collective hibernation sleep to undo the earth's natural cycle of changes. In the game, we control seven dreamers who, through an error, go together to the same lucid dream. Their task is to unravel the mystery of the world in which they are located and gain the right amount of awareness points to wake up from the limbo.

Game available at: https://lazycoastgames.itch.io/pyxis

My responsibilites in the project included:

  • Designing multiple platformer levels in Tiled
  • Working on the story - coming up with several ideas implemented in the
    game
  • Playing the piano parts of the soundtrack
  • Drawing and animating numerous 16 x 16 px pixel art sprites

The Process

The world of PYXIS sets a rather "dreamy" vibe, which meant that the levels needed to fit the game aesthetic. Each level starts with a famous painting converted to pixel art that suits the level's overall feeling. Additionally, each level represents one dream and as we all know dreams can be really abstract - we tried to depict that in the game levels and soundtrack.

PYXIS had its own premiere event at Regionalne Centrum Kultury in Kołobrzeg where we could present our game to a broader audience - including the President of Kołobrzeg.
