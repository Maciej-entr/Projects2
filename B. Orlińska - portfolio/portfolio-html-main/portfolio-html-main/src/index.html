Hi I'm Beatka

Aspiring Level Designer | Girls in the Game! alumni | University student

I'm an aspiring Level Designer and a university student at the beginning of my professional journey, currently focused on developing a game called "Long Time to Sea" and leading a team of 8 people in the "Cyberiada" competition. In 2023 I became a mentee of the "Girls in the Game!" program run by CD PROJEKT RED and Perspektywy Women in Tech. I gained one year of private 1:1 mentorship with CDPR experts during which I've properly discovered Level Design and started working on my first projects in Unreal Engine 5. What got me into the program was my activity in Lazy Coast Games which is the only (from what I know) indie game studio for youth (with members aged 11-19) which I have joined in September 2020.
